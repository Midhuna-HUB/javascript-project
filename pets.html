💥 Let's Look About The Ability and Skins Of The Pets 😎...!!

🦊 Spirit Fox Ability:

  • Restores an extra 4 HP when using healing items like medkits.
  • Provides additional health recovery, making healing more effective during fights.
🎨 Spirit Fox Skins:
  • Features a sleek silver-white fur with glowing blue accents, giving it a mystical, icy look.
  • Covered in fiery red-orange fur with ember-like patterns, making the fox look fierce and energetic.

🐒 Beaston Ability:

  • Increases the throwing distance of grenades, flashbangs, and gloo walls by 30%.
  • Useful for aggressive gameplay and long-distance grenade attacks.

🎨 Beaston Skins:
  • Features a rugged gorilla look with military-style accessories.
  • Special jungle-themed skin with green camouflage patterns.

🐼 Detective Panda Ability:

  • Restores 10 HP after every kill.
  • Perfect for aggressive players who love close combat fights.
🎨 Detective Panda Skins:
  • Classic detective outfit with a stylish hat and coat.
  • Special black-and-white suit skin for a formal look.

🦅 Falco Ability:

  • Increases gliding speed by 15% and diving speed after parachute opens by 25%.
  • Helps players land faster and get loot quickly.
🎨 Falco Skins:
  • Features vibrant blue feathers with golden highlights.
  • Flaming red and orange skin that looks like a phoenix.

🐱 Kitty Ability:

  • Kitty is a decorative pet without any special abilities.
  • Used purely for aesthetic purposes in the game.
🎨 Kitty Skins:
  • Cute pink outfit with a bowtie and bell collar.
  • Adorable black-and-white tuxedo cat skin.

🐧 Mr. Waggor Ability:

  • Produces 1 gloo wall grenade every 100 seconds when you have less than 2 gloo walls.
  • Excellent for defensive gameplay and cover creation.
🎨 Mr. Waggor Skins:
  • Classic tuxedo with a bowtie, giving it a formal look.
  • Colorful summer-themed skin with sunglasses and beachwear.

🐯 Sensei Tig Ability:

  • Reduces the duration of enemy's marked skills by 60% (like Moco's mark).
  • Useful for avoiding enemy tracking and improving stealth.
🎨 Sensei Tig Skins:
  • Traditional tiger warrior outfit with samurai elements.
  • Bright red armor skin with gold highlights for a fierce look.

🐕 Shiba Ability:

  • Marks nearby mushrooms every 120 seconds on the map.
  • Helps players collect EP (energy points) easily.
🎨 Shiba Skins:
  • Classic orange Shiba Inu look with a cute scarf.
  • Cool ninja-themed black outfit for a stealthy vibe.
